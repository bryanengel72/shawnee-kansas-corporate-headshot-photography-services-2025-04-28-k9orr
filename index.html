Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to my headshot session?

We recommend solid colors and classic styles that align with your industry. Bring 2-3 outfit options, and we'll help you select the most flattering choices. Avoid busy patterns, bright colors, and trendy styles that may quickly date your photos.

How long does a typical session take?

Individual sessions usually last 45-60 minutes, allowing time for outfit changes and multiple poses. Brand sessions can run 2-3 hours to capture a comprehensive set of images. Our live feedback system ensures efficient use of your time.

When will I receive my final images?

You'll receive a curated gallery of edited photos within 3-5 business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee. All images include basic retouching and are delivered in both high-resolution and web-optimized formats.

Do you offer hair and makeup services?

Yes, we partner with professional hair and makeup artists who specialize in camera-ready looks. This service can be added to any package and is highly recommended for the best results.

What about licensing and usage rights?

All packages include full personal and professional usage rights for your images. You're free to use them on websites, social media, marketing materials, and press releases. Additional licensing options are available for corporate use.
